Who I Am

I'm an entry-level cybersecurity professional grounded in mathematics and computer science, with hands-on experience in cloud security, SIEM deployment, threat detection, and malware analysis. My journey began with a deep curiosity about how systems fail — and how to make them not.

My home lab is where theory meets practice: I've built a custom Python SIEM tool from scratch, deployed a honeypot on a cloud VM to track real attacker behaviour, and engineered a multi-threaded port scanner. Each project is documented and open on GitHub.
